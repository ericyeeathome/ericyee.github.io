Montara Beach

After a hike up some rocks I had a beautiful view of the sky right after sunset as well as the crashing waves along the beach. This is probably one of my favorite spots in the bay area.

  • December 2014

Thailand

This sunset was taken while kayaking on mangroves while in Thailand. We were in two person kayaks and had to paddle out a mile before we got into open water. It was low tide so sometimes we got stuck in mud and had to carry the kayak out.

  • August 2014

Santa Cruz

This photo was taken after surfing in Pleasure Point, Santa Cruz. The waves were pretty small, but the session was pretty fun since I had not surfed for a while. It was fun just being in the water and enjoying the scenery.
